about me

I am a product manager and software engineer who thrives at the intersection of ideas and people. I earned my MBA at Johns Hopkins University Carey Business School and my BA at Wellesley College, and currently work at the technology consulting firm Booz Allen Hamilton in Washington, DC. I have worked on a range of projects for government agencies like the VA, NIH, and DOL / DOI, as well as on commercial products like Sailfish and District Defend. In a previous life, I wrote copy and edited articles for a small educational nonprofit.

In my spare time, I like to read, practice yoga, grow plants/herbs, cook, travel (both near and far), ride my bike, take photos, and watch movies.
