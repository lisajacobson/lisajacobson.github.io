about me

I am a product manager and software engineer who thrives at the intersection of ideas and people. I have an MBA and I work at the technology consulting firm Booz Allen Hamilton in Washington, DC. I have worked on a range of projects for government agencies like the VA, NIH, and DOL / DOI, as well as commercial products like Sailfish and District Defend. In a previous life, I wrote copy and edited articles for a small educational nonprofit.

In my spare time, I like to read, watch movies, travel, bike ride, take photos, and hang out with loved ones.
