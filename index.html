Portfolio



iCook

Recipes based on what you already have.

iCook is a cooking app that gives you recipes you can make based on ingredients you already have. It was developed as a sample Rails project for the August 2014 Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly in a one-week sprint to model the agile software development process. iCook uses the Yummly recipe aggregator API.

Users can search recipes based on ingredients they already have, and save their favorite recipes.

SpeakUp

Eradicating school bullying one concern at a time.

Speakup is an application for middle and high school students and counselors to combat bullying. SpeakUp was developed as an individual Rails project for the August 2014 Web Development Immersive Course at General Assembly DC. It was developed over a four-day sprint to model the agile software development process including Git version control and daily SCRUMs.

Users can log concerns and school administrators can respond to student concerns.

Mappetizer

Harnessing the power of maps to enhance your group outings

Mappetizer is a social web application that enables friends to more effectively plan group outings. It was developed as the group project during WDI in a 4-day spring modeling the agile software development process including frequent SCRUMs. Mappetizer utilizes the Eventbrite and Mapbox APIs to return event results and location markers on the map for the user-selected date/time range.

Users can log in, choose a date and time range, view all events in that frame, and add events to their outings, which they can name and further customize to their liking.
