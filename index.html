about me

I am a product manager and software engineer who thrives at the intersection of ideas and people. I earned my MBA at Johns Hopkins University Carey Business School and my BA at Wellesley College, and currently work at the technology consulting firm Booz Allen Hamilton in Washington, DC. I have helped design and built platforms and prototypes for government agencies like the VA, the NIH, and DOL - to name a few - and commercial products such as Sailfish and District Defend. In a previous life, I wrote copy and edited articles for a small educational nonprofit and various publications.

Aside from doing work that I love, I enjoy: doing puzzles like the NYT daily mini crossword puzzle; practicing yoga; gardening; cooking; and the perennial profile "spare time activity," *traveling.
